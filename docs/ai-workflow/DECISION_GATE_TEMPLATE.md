# Decision Gate Template

Use this before any non-trivial implementation.

## 1. Proposed change
What exactly are we changing?

## 2. Why now?
What problem does this solve?
Is it blocking launch, quality, cost, UX, or maintainability?

## 3. Scope
Is this:
- General system change
- Story-specific config/data
- One-off debug/test change

## 4. Risk of hardcoding
Could this accidentally solve only one story, one child, one companion, or one page?

## 5. Files likely affected
List files/modules expected to change.

## 6. Expected behavior after change
Describe the desired behavior in plain language.

## 7. Validation plan
What is the smallest test that proves this works?
Do not run a full book unless explicitly approved.

## 8. Cost impact
Does this spend image/audio/API money?
How many generations are expected?

## 9. Rollback plan
How do we undo this safely?

## 10. What ChatGPT should review
What strategic/product/QA questions need review before implementation?

## 11. Do not do
Explicit forbidden actions.
