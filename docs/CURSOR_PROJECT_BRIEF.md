# SMALL HEROES — PROJECT BRIEF

## Overview

Small Heroes is a product that creates **personalized emotional stories for children (ages 3–10)**.

The purpose is to help children process emotional challenges through:

* metaphor
* imagination
* safe storytelling
* emotional identification

This is NOT a generic AI story generator.

This IS:

* a personalized emotional experience
* built around what the child is actually going through
* designed for parents
* delivered as a premium storybook experience

---

## Core Philosophy

We do NOT explain emotions directly.

We use:

* symbolic characters
* imaginative worlds
* emotional mirroring

Example:
Not:
"You are afraid of loud noises"

Instead:
A giant who makes loud stomping sounds because he is lost and searching for his parents.

---

## Target Audience

Parents of children aged 3–10
Primary focus: ages 4–8

Common situations:

* fear at night
* loud noises / sirens
* separation
* social difficulty
* low confidence
* emotional overwhelm
* anger
* transitions

---

## UX Principles

The product must feel:

* simple
* safe
* calm
* premium
* fast

Rules:

* short steps
* short text
* minimal friction
