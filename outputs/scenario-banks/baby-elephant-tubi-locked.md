## S1 — "היריד" (adventure, 12) — navigate by ONE sound the child makes/owns

- trigger: packed evening fair — calls, wheels, music, laughter at once. childProblem: flooded at the gate; can't reach the one ride they came for. misread: "אם אכנס, הכול ייכנס בבת אחת."
- companionEntry: Tubi wedged behind a too-small ticket booth, ears flung wide catching everything; his foot makes a בּוּם that startles him.
- engineUse: Tubi folds ears half, picks the ride's bell.
- **childAgency (per ChatGPT — child's OWN sound):** the child picks a sound *they* make/own — the click of their own shoes on the boards, or a quiet count tapped on the parent's hand — and follows THAT thread through the crowd. Not passively trailing a parent.
- climax: they reach the ride by tracking their own chosen thread across the noise.
- comicBeat: Tubi declares "אני רגוע לגמרי" while his ears flap like two curtains in a storm; one ear still secretly chases a far-off cart ("אני שומע רק קול אחד" — while an ear drifts after the cart).
- imagery: ear-curtains half; a thin "path of one sound" through a sea of sound-shapes. residue: the fair stays loud; the child keeps their chosen sound, the rest is "גשם מעבר לחלון."
- whyThisIsFresh: moving crowd + a self-owned moving sound = navigation; distinct from the static settings.

## S2 — "הבית בלילה" (bedtime, 8) — pick one small kind sound to rest (leads with too-SHUT)

- trigger: house at night — fridge hum, wind, far car, hallway creak. childProblem: can't sleep; every small sound yanks attention; dark makes ears feel huge. misread: "אם אקשיב אשמע משהו רע; אם אסגור אפספס את אמא."
- companionEntry: Tubi under the bed, ears clamped shut like heavy curtains — so shut he doesn't hear the child say hello (the too-SHUT failure leads here).
- engineUse: Tubi draws the ear-curtains to the middle, picks one small kind sound — the clock's slow tick.
- childAgency: the child chooses their OWN one small sound — a soft tick / their own slow steps earlier echoing / Tubi's quiet count — half-ear, not shut.
- **anti-fawn guard (ChatGPT):** keep it EARS / SOUND / CHOICE. NOT "soft breath / a soft thing to hold" sensory-regulation — that's fawn_tzvi. Tubi's tool is choosing a sound through half-open ears, not finding a soothing texture.
- climax: the loud night becomes background; one gentle chosen sound stays close. comicBeat: "הבטן שלי שמעה את זה לפניי," after his own stomach rumbles at the fridge. imagery: curtain-ears at half; one soft sound glowing small in a dark room. residue: the house still hums; the child keeps one small sound and sleeps.
- whyThisIsFresh: rest/soothing selection; the too-SHUT failure leads (vs too-open elsewhere).

## S4 — "הרעם" (bedtime, 8) — choose the rain's tap between booms (uncontrollable source)

- trigger: thunderstorm at night — cracks you cannot predict or stop. childProblem/misread: "אם אני לא יכול לעצור את זה, אני חסר אונים."
- companionEntry: Tubi's ears fling wide at the first crack; he catches the whole storm at once, eyes huge, belly רְרוּם.
- engineUse: instead of the thunder, Tubi picks the steady טַף־טַף of rain and the quiet GAP between cracks.
- childAgency: can't control the thunder — chooses what to listen TO: the rain's tap / the calm gap. Control where the ears rest, not the outcome.
- **FORBIDDEN (ChatGPT, hard — anti-whale):** NO "מממ"/humming, NO lullaby/melody, NO turning the thunder into a rhythm or a pleasant sound, NO "every big sound has a song." The thunder STAYS thunder. Tool literally: "הרעם קיים. אני בוחר את טַף־טַף של הגשם בין הבּוּמים."
- climax: a big crack comes — the child has already chosen the rain underneath it; the boom passes "מעבר לחלון." comicBeat: "כף אחת עשתה בּוּם, וטוּבִּי קפץ מהבּוּם של עצמו" — can't tell his own thump from thunder. residue: the storm rolls on; the child holds the rain-sound + the gaps.
- whyThisIsFresh: UNCONTROLLABLE source — fear is helplessness; agency = choosing focus, not outcome. (Use SECOND in validation, after the engine is proven, due to whale-proximity.)

## S5 — "הזיקוקים" (adventure, 12) — STAY and enjoy a WANTED-but-loud thing on your own terms

- trigger: fireworks / an Independence-Day show / parade the child genuinely WANTS to see — but the booms are too big. childProblem: desire vs flood (not pure fear). misread: "או שאני נהנה ומוצף, או שאני בורח ומפספס."
- companionEntry: Tubi covers his EYES by mistake instead of his ears, then flings ears wide at the first boom.
- engineUse: Tubi keeps eyes ON the colors, ears at half — booms stay "מעבר לחלון" while he watches.
- **childAgency (per ChatGPT — fuller):** the child DECIDES where to stand, WHEN to look, and WHAT to let into their ears — eyes on the lights, ears at half. Active staging, not just "staying."
- climax: the biggest boom + brightest color together — the child watches, ears half, keeps the moment. comicBeat: "האוזניים שלי פתחו את כל הדלתות" — right as he meant to keep them half. imagery: ear-curtains half against bursting color; booms drawn as shapes held at the window's far side. residue: the show ends; the child kept a wanted thing without being flooded out of it.
- whyThisIsFresh: the loud thing is DESIRED — desire-vs-overwhelm, not fear. The most product-resonant scenario → validation pick #1.

## S6 — "השקט שנשבר" (bedtime, 8) — INVERSION: one sudden sound + recover AFTER the startle

- trigger (bedtime framing per ChatGPT): a too-quiet house after everyone's asleep — then a toy falls / a door closes / a cough in the hallway cracks the hush. childProblem: in the hush, braced for the one unpredictable sound; when it comes, ears fling wide and flood. misread: "בשקט הגדול, כל קול פתאומי הוא ענק."
- companionEntry: Tubi holding his breath to be silent — and his trunk does a tiny embarrassed פּוּף in the quiet.
- engineUse: after the sudden sound, Tubi RE-FOLDS ears to half instead of staying flung-open, picks one steady sound (the clock, his own breath-count).
- childAgency: the child RECOVERS — after the startle, chooses to re-fold to half and pick one calm sound, instead of staying flooded/braced.
- climax: a second sudden sound comes — this time the child re-folds fast and stays steady. comicBeat: "החדק שלי עשה פּוּף בלי לשאול אותי." imagery: ear-curtains snapping wide then drawn back to half; a sudden sound-shape that shrinks as it's let go. residue: the quiet returns; the child knows how to come back to half after a startle.
- whyThisIsFresh: INVERSION — quiet not loud; problem is a single unpredictable sound + RECOVERY (a new mechanism: re-fold, not manage-continuous).
- note: if framed as library/class instead of night-house, reclassify as adventure/social. Keep bedtime = night-house version.
