# bolly_armadillo — scenario bank LOCKED (ChatGPT-reviewed, edits applied)

`companionId: bolly_armadillo` · Bolly · male · `category: SELF_CONFIDENCE`. 4 active + 1 reserve. Validation = Bolly × non-golden directions (bedtime + adventure), which also sidesteps the parked fantasy-wonder lift.

**TypeScript source:** `lib/story-gen/scenarios-bolly-armadillo.ts`

## Engine (locked)

**"come out of your shell — one peek counts."** Shell = a HOME you can leave AND return to (never something to get rid of). Confidence = peeking out a bit more each time, on your OWN terms. Signature: roll/curl, קליק, nose→eye→step peek, deadpan-brave from inside the ball. Refrains: "הצצה אחת נחשבת" · "לא כל בולי בבת אחת" · "אני רועד קטן" · "אפשר לחזור לקליפה — זה לא מוחק את ההצצה."

## QA line on EVERY scenario (sharpened, ChatGPT)

**Pass:** the child chooses a SMALL exposure themselves, can step BACK, and stepping back does NOT erase the try. **Fail:** someone pushes the child forward · the child is "cured" all at once · the shell is shown as something to get rid of · the child coaxes Bolly out (role-reversal, forbidden).

## Per-scenario rolling comedy (REQUIRED)

Each scenario carries an irreplaceable armadillo physical beat: rolls one step too far and back · raises a brave "hand" still inside the ball · says "אני לגמרי בחוץ" with only his nose out · "התקדמות עגולה היא גם התקדמות" · a too-dramatic קְליק the kids think is part of the game.

## B1 — "להצטרף למשחק" (adventure, 12) — SOCIAL ENTRY — **validation pick #1**

- trigger: a group of kids already playing; the child wants in but freezes at the edge. misread: "אם אתקרב, כולם יסתכלו עליי בבת אחת."
- companionEntry: Bolly rolls up, bumps the child's shoe — "הגעתי במצב עגול" — narrating bravely from inside the ball.
- engineUse: nose (watch from afar) → eye (step closer) → foot (stand at the edge).
- **childAgency:** the child lets themselves be SEEN at the edge of the game without being swallowed by everyone's gaze — and can step back and in again. Climax: the child asks ONE kid, not the whole group — "אפשר גם אני?" = *לא כל הילד בבת אחת.*
- **distinct from panda_anat:** panda = my rhythm creates a place; Bolly = I can be seen a little, step in a little, and return if I need.
- comicBeat: Bolly rolls one body-length toward the group, then back to the start — "התקדמות עגולה היא גם התקדמות"; his foot trembles, "אני רועד קטן."
- residue: the child is in the game and can step out/in again — "הצצה אחת נחשבת."

## B2 — "המילה שלי" (adventure, 12) — VOICE EXPOSURE (public)

- trigger: the child has one line / a short song / their name to say in front of others. misread: "אם אדבר, הקול ייצא רועד וכולם יראו."
- companionEntry: Bolly, curled, rehearses the line from INSIDE the ball in a tiny brave voice — "אני מוכן לגמרי" (still rolled).
- engineUse: mouth closed → one word → a small sentence → can return to the shell. Fear = trembling VOICE, not medical ordeal.
- childAgency: the child says it in their OWN peeks — mouths it, then one word aloud, then the small line — their voice, their pace.
- comicBeat: Bolly delivers the line flawlessly from inside the shell; when it's his turn out loud, only a "קְליק" comes out — "הקול שלי עוד מתחמם."
- residue: the child said their part; next time one word more — "לא כל בולי בבת אחת."

## B4 — "החדר שלי בלילה" (bedtime, 8) — CONFIDENCE WITHOUT AUDIENCE — **validation pick #2**

- trigger: the child wants to be "big" and settle in their own room / their own space at night, but feels small in the dark — confidence with no one watching. misread: "אם אני לבד, אני קטן."
- companionEntry: Bolly curls snug on the bed — **"הקליפה היא בית, לא מחבוא"** — modeling the shell as a home you can leave and return to.
- engineUse: Bolly peeks out into the dark room, then back in — out a bit more — leaving-and-returning is allowed.
- **childAgency:** small brave thing — stays in their own bed a few minutes, peeks into the dark, leaves the door open, KNOWS they can return / call a parent and that returning does NOT erase the try.
- comicBeat: Bolly announces "אני ישֵן באומץ מלא," then peeks one eye to check the room is still there — "העולם עדיין שם. טוב"; tries to "raise a brave hand" but it's still inside the ball.
- residue: the child stayed in their space; "אפשר לחזור לקליפה — זה לא מוחק את ההצצה."

## B5 — "המשפט הקטן שלי" (bedtime, 8) — VOICE (private, almost-pressed-out)

- trigger: the child has been holding in a tiny personal want/feeling and is afraid to say it even to a parent. misread: "אם אגיד, זה יישמע קטן/מטופש."
- companionEntry: Bolly whispers the brave version from inside the ball — "אני אגיד את זה ברגע" — staying rolled.
- engineUse: the words peek out softly — half-said → said.
- **childAgency:** a very small, almost-pressed-out personal sentence — NOT a little speech. Tiny, intimate, low-stakes.
- comicBeat: Bolly rehearses a grand speech inside the shell, then out loud says only "...כן." and is very proud — "התחלה מצוינת"; "אני לגמרי בחוץ" while only his nose shows.
