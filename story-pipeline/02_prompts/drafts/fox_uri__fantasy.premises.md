## 1 · The door that appears at night
- **premise:** each night a small glowing door shows up in {{childName}}'s room that isn't there by day. Mystery: what's behind it?
- **central object:** the little night-door. **clues accumulate:** a draft, a smell of rain, one feather beneath it, a soft tick.
- **recurring gag:** Uri names it grandly ("דלת הרפאים!"), approaches "אבל לא בהגזמה," lantern flickers, tail hides.
- **emotional mistake:** {{childName}} tries to shove the door fully open at once (light all the dark) → it feels scarier.
- **child agency:** {{childName}} notices the feather (the clue Uri missed), opens the door a crack, lights "just a little."
- **why NOT generic:** lantern-logic + inspectable fear; behind it is a cozy night-bird nook, not a monster.
- **risk:** keep it cozy; one door, not a portal-quest.
- **score:** fresh 7 · truth 7 · fit 8 → **7.4**

## 2 · The hallway that stretches  ⭐
- **premise:** at night the hallway to the bathroom keeps growing longer (magical). Mystery: why does it stretch, and what's at the end?
- **central object:** a small lantern-lit doorknob at the far end. **clues:** each step a new thing appears — a lamp, a little painting, a draft — and the paintings show {{childName}}'s own day.
- **recurring gag:** Uri "measures" the hallway dramatically, miscounts, lantern shrinks when he's scared.
- **emotional mistake:** {{childName}} runs faster to reach the end → the hallway grows *more*.
- **child agency:** {{childName}} notices the paintings-are-my-day clue → lights a little and takes one small step, and the hallway *shrinks*.
- **why NOT generic:** "lighting a little shrinks the worry" is Uri's exact gift, literalized as a mystery with clues.
- **risk:** keep the "stretch = worry" gentle, never nightmarish.
- **score:** fresh 8 · truth 8 · fit 9 → **8.3**

## 4 · The toy that grew giant in the dark
- **premise:** at night one toy becomes large and shadowy (magical). Mystery: is it the toy, or something else?
- **central object:** the toy. **clues:** it's the right shape, it has the toy's one missing ear, it doesn't move when you do.
- **recurring gag:** Uri "investigates" from odd angles, names "ענק הצעצוע".
- **emotional mistake:** {{childName}} hides under the blanket (don't look) → the giant looms longer.

- **score:** fresh 6 · truth 7 · fit 7 → **6.7**

## 5 · The little stairs under the bed  ⭐
- **premise:** under the bed opens a tiny stairway of small lights going down into a soft dark (magical). Mystery: where do they lead + what's the sound at the bottom?
- **central object:** the stair-lights. **clues:** each star lights a piece, a soft snore, a warm draft.
- **recurring gag:** Uri cries "מדרגות התהום!" then de-escalates to "טוב… מדרגות קטנות."
- **emotional mistake:** {{childName}} slams the gap shut (avoid) → the sound gets louder in the imagination.
- **child agency:** {{childName}} notices the snore is *gentle* → lights one step at a time, goes "just a little."
- **why NOT generic:** the classic under-bed fear made inspectable; the dark is *sleeping*, not hunting.
- **risk:** keep the descent tiny and safe, never a real plunge.
- **score:** fresh 7 · truth 8 · fit 9 → **8.0**

## 6 · The night that pours in like water  ⭐⭐
- **premise:** when the lamp goes off, the dark "pours" into the room like rising water (magical, a child's literal dark-fear). Mystery: how deep is it, and what's floating in it?
- **central object:** the lantern as a small island/boat of light. **clues:** shapes float by and Uri names each — a slipper-boat, a fish-sock, a paper-moon.
- **recurring gag:** Uri "captains" the lantern-boat, lantern dims when a big shape passes.
- **emotional mistake:** {{childName}} believes the dark is deep as the sea and freezes → it feels bottomless.
- **child agency:** {{childName}} lights a little and discovers the "deep" dark is only ankle-deep → wades a small path across.
- **why NOT generic:** literalizes Uri's whole thesis — "don't light all the dark, light a little" — as one mystery with floating clues. Only the lantern-meter carries it.
- **risk:** keep "water" playful, never drowning/scary.
- **score:** fresh 9 · truth 8 · fit 10 → **8.7**
