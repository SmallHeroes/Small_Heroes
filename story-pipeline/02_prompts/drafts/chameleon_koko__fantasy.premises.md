# Premise Lab · `chameleon_koko` (קים) · fantasy (8 candidates) — light guardrails

TRANSITION · 16 beats · age 4–6. ⭐ mandatory-spine slot.
**Guardrails (light):** Kim does NOT camouflage/disappear to belong — she brings her color. Concrete **home-token**. Weak-direction note: a magical world is fine, but the message is *carry home*, not *adapt away*. Score = input, you pick.
**Anchor:** a world that shifts/changes (transition literalized); the constant is what you carry.
