## 3. The strategic problem (why the mechanism exists)
The brand promise is "a real premium children's book that happens to be personalized." The risk: generating the remaining ~80 stories from `DeepProfile + direction + golden few-shot` would make the model imitate structure → flat, recycled-feeling variations → exactly the templated feel we sell against. Empirically (the koko A/B) the recipe→LLM pipeline tops out at "competent compliant prose," not literary tier. So the mechanism must protect against both **templating** (same shape, different animal) and **mediocrity** (correct but boring).

---

## 4. The mechanism — design (Decision Gate APPROVED, Option A)
Text/structure only, **zero image spend**, **advisory-only** (no auto-reroll/reject until a separate future phase). Four components:
1. **Per-companion scenario banks** — 5–8 distinct, pre-authored scenarios per companion (data, not code). The biggest anti-template lever. Authored by Claude/ChatGPT, never model-dumped.
2. **freshnessTest** (advisory) — 15-dimension overlap report vs the corpus. **Critical rule:** a companion's *signature engine* recurring is REQUIRED (swapTest wants it); only repeated *story shape* is penalized. (Two channels: craft judges prose engine-blind; freshness/swap judge shape.)
3. **swapTest** (advisory) — is the story bound to this companion's engine, or could a generic animal carry it?
4. **craftRubricTest** — MANDATORY literary/commercial quality judge. "Fresh but bad" is the failure freshness/swap miss.

Flow is **outline-first**: `scenario → outline → outline-level swap+freshness → prose → validators → swap → freshness → craft → advisory report`.

## 5. The craft judge — built & validated (v1 → v2 → v2.1)
This was the linchpin and the hardest part.
- **v1** (absolute 0–10): separated goldens (9) from obvious decoys (3–4) — but the **middle-band probe failed**: it scored a "competent but not golden" generated story (Phase-A Bolly) at **9.0**, same as real goldens. It rewarded polished text, couldn't tell mediocre from golden, and cited English imageDirections as prose evidence.
- **v2** (comparative/anchored + prose-only + weakest-line + humor/companion/orality caps): fixed the middle band (Phase-A Bolly 9.0 → 7.1). BUT a contamination bug surfaced — the real `bolly_armadillo_fantasy` golden dropped to 7.6 because a mid anchor card shared Bolly's *engine vocabulary*.
- **v2.1** (quality anchors made **companion-agnostic** + explicit "engine vocabulary ≠ quality" + `borderline-golden` band) — **PASSED** (commit `e5b7ac0b`). Test A (remove contaminating card): Bolly golden 7.6→9.1 = contamination proven & fixed. Test B (masked engine vocab): Bolly golden 9.1 vs Phase-A 7.4, justified by prose only. Ladder clean: 10 goldens 9.1–9.2, mid anchors 6.8–7.3, decoys (the clean one) ~4.

**craft-rubric-v2.1 is the official craft judge. Do not modify it.** v1/v2 are historical only.

**Known debt (not advisory-blocking):** 2 of 3 decoys (Talking Turtle, Soft Friend) land ~6.8/competent-not-golden rather than decoy — they're "competent-flat," which legitimately overlaps the mid band; only the hard-fail decoy (Owl) stays clearly decoy. Nothing climbs to golden, so trash can't pass as golden. Must sharpen (rule: decoy = hard-fail OR very-low; add 1–2 truly-broken decoys) **before any auto-block (future Phase D)**, not before advisory use.
